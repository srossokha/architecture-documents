### 1. Visit Planer
> You will need to develop a single-page React application featuring a map of your neighborhood or a neighborhood you would like to visit. Your map will identify popular local places and include a search. You'll also incorporate a third-party API (Google, Booking and etc.) to provide additional information about each of these locations.
